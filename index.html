The product

5 workflows. 10 agents.
One operator in control.

1

Lead Discovery & Outreach

Operator defines ideal customer profile. Lead Discovery searches LinkedIn. Lead Enrichment qualifies. Email Drafter creates personalized messages. Everything waits for approval.

2

Response & Follow-up Management

Monitors incoming replies, detects intent, auto-updates the CRM, drafts follow-ups in the operator's voice. All queued for approval before send.

3

Remote Meeting Assistant

Joins Zoom/Teams calls, transcribes in real-time, generates internal summary + client memo, saves both to CRM automatically.

4

Prospect Strategy on Demand

Operator types a name. Profile Intelligence pulls LinkedIn data via Ninja Pear, builds personalized closing strategy, generates Word document and outreach email draft, delivered via email.

Profile Intelligence — Data Stack & GDPR▼

Data stack — MVP1

Ninja Pear — LinkedIn profile scraping. Career history, current role, education, skills, public activity. GDPR-safe.

Coresignal — B2B professional data. Company signals, employee count, tech stack, industry classification.

Tavily — Real-time web research. News, press releases, recent public mentions of the prospect or company.

Crunchbase — Investment history, funding rounds, notable deals. Public data only.

What stays out

  • FB/IG scraping — violates Meta ToS, requires legal review
  • Prospect location/behavioral data — GDPR restrictions
  • Bank data — requires PSD2 compliance + formal legal review

Financial Advisor — Inputs & PSD2 Status▼

Data inputs (MVP2)

  • CRM deal history: past deal sizes, close rates, payment history
  • Profile signals: company size, industry, seniority, years in role
  • Public data: funding rounds (Crunchbase), press releases

Bank connections — blocked until legal sign-off

Operator bank data (Plaid US / Tink EU) would add cash flow context. PSD2 compliance in Europe requires formal legal review before any build decision.
